# Miranda IControl General - HLP-1801

The **Miranda IControl General - HLP-1801** connector monitors a lip-sync probe module.

## About

This connector is automatically generated by the connector Miranda IControl General, range 2.0.0.x.

### Product Info

| Range | Supported Firmware Version |
|------------------|-----------------------------|
| 2.0.0.x          | Unknown                     |

## Installation and configuration

### Creation

This connector is used by DVE child elements that are **automatically created** by the parent connector [Miranda IControl General](xref:Connector_help_Miranda_IControl_General), from version 2.0.0.x onwards.

## Usage

### General

This page contains general information about the module: **Overall Status**, **Card LED**, **Input 1 & 2 Status**, **Lipsync Group** and **Video Delay**.

### Audio

On this page, you can find information regarding **Input 1 - All Channels** and **Input 2 - All Channels**.

### Presence

This page displays the **Lipsync** **Delay** and **Audio** **Match**.

### Status

This page displays information on the **Lipsync** **Channels**.
